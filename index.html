Håkon Hoel's homepage

I work as a Junior Professor at the Chair of Mathematics for Uncertainty Quantification, RWTH Aachen. My main research interests are numerical analysis of stochastic differential equations, nonlinear filtering and Monte Carlo methods. For further details, here is my CV.



Current teaching:

Mathematics and numerics for data assimilation and state estimation, summer semester 2020.
